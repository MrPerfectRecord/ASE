hero.mp4
========

Currently in place: aerial drone shot of a glass-curtain-wall tower at
sunrise (Chicago), 1280x720, ~24s loop, ~8.5 MB.

Source: Pexels video #7101177 by Gustavo Fring
        https://www.pexels.com/video/drone-shot-of-tall-buildings-7101177/
License: Pexels License — free for commercial use, no attribution required.

To swap it for something else, just replace this file with another
`hero.mp4` (H.264 .mp4, ideally 720p or 1080p, under ~10 MB, no audio
track needed). The hero element falls back to `/home-hero.png` automatically
if the video is missing or fails to load.

Other good free sources:
  - Pexels   https://www.pexels.com/videos/
  - Mixkit   https://mixkit.co/free-stock-video/
  - Coverr   https://coverr.co/
